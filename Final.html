Deal or No Deal

Our game is a Deal or No Deal game, you start off on a splash screen that is
animated when you are ready click the start game button with your mouse.
You will then be brought to the main screen where you are to eliminate 6
boxes by clicking on each box you want to eliminate the number. As soon as the 6
boxes are clicked a next screen will appear that offers the user a deal depending
on the total amount of money left in the boxes you havenâ€™t eliminated. You then
have to make the choice to take the deal where you will be brought to a scree
that shows you how much money you eliminated in the first round and the game will
be over. Or click no deal and you will bring back to the main screen and eliminate
5 more boxes. The game will repeat in this style eliminating 6,5,5,4,3,2,1 with a
new deal between each round getting progressively higher in value. You can either
take the deal or be given on box and you receive whatever is in that box no matter
the value.
